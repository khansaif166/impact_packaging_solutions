What Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it to us through our website contact form. The information collected includes:

  • Full name
  • Company name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Product interest and requirements
  • Any additional information you include in your message

We do not collect any information automatically through cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools that identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry or quote request
  • To understand your packaging requirements
  • To follow up on your business enquiry
  • To provide you with relevant product information

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing communications. We do not sell, rent, trade or share your personal information with any third party for their marketing purposes.

Third Party Services

Our website uses the following third party services that may process data as part of their normal operation:

Web3Forms (web3forms.com)

We use Web3Forms to process contact form submissions. When you submit our contact form, your data is transmitted through Web3Forms' servers to reach our email inbox. Web3Forms' privacy policy is available at their website.

Cloudflare (cloudflare.com)

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process technical data including IP addresses as part of providing hosting and security services. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available at their website.

Google Fonts (fonts.google.com)

Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts. This may result in your browser making a request to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy is available at their website.

None of these services are authorized to use your personal information for any purpose other than providing their services to us.

Data Retention

We retain enquiry information received through our contact form only for as long as necessary to respond to and follow up on your business enquiry. If a business relationship is established, relevant information may be retained as part of our business records in compliance with applicable Indian law.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you share with us. Our website is served over HTTPS and our contact form data is transmitted securely. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

As a visitor to our website, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
